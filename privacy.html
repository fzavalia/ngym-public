Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This policy explains what data Ngym collects — and what it does not.

Data Collection

Ngym does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — workouts, exercises, and sets — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Third-Party Services

Ngym does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not sell your data.

iCloud

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Apple may sync your data across your own devices through your personal iCloud account. This data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Ngym is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
